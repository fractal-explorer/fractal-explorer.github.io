FRACTAL EXPLORER

Fractal Explorer utilizes algorithmic art to combine elements of music, math, and psychology to create a moving virtual reality experience. The user chooses a song, and are placed within an immersive animated, recursively generated three-dimensional fractal that changes in appearance to complement the song.
